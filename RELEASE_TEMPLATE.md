## Distribution (for Java)

```xml
<repositories>
    <repository>
        <id>repo.grakn.ai</id>
        <url>https://repo.grakn.ai/repository/maven/</url>
    </repository>
</repositories>

<dependencies>
    <dependency>
        <groupId>io.graql</groupId>
        <artifactId>graql-lang</artifactId>
        <version>{version}</version>
    </dependency>
</dependencies>
```
